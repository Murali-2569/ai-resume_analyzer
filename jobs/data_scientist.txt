Job Title: Data Scientist

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to extract insights and predict business outcomes
- Design machine learning models for regression, classification, clustering
- Work with Python, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, Pandas
- Develop dashboards and visualizations (using Tableau/Power BI)
- Collaborate with stakeholders to define analytics requirements

Requirements:
- Proficient in SQL, Python, machine learning techniques, and data visualization
- Experience with large datasets and statistical models
- Strong communication and analytical skills
